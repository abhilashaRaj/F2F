Farmers cite other farmers as a valued source of information. They rely upon the knowledge and experience of other farmers who are in situations similar to their own. Their desire to meet and discuss ideas has led to formation of discussion groups. Traditionally, these groups have been mere informal gatherings. We have come up with an idea to facilitate an e-portal for such discussions and information sharing among farmers. Proposed technology will transform the old agriculture system and provide the new venture to farmers which will improve the productivity, market value and proper marketing setup with youth employment. Precision agriculture demonstrates what is needed, where is needed, how we are defined. The proper setup is explained through the green revolution. We can improve the agriculture systems with the help of available latest communication technology such as 4G/ 5G and beyond and existing modern resources in the market. By forming and facilitating e-learning groups, we can continue to serve the agriculture community.
